Design Brainstorming

Summary of ideas generated during initial and subsequent design brainstorming sessions.

Leg Configuration: 2 DOF vs. 3 DOF

Evaluated 2 DOF vs 3 DOF leg configurations. 3 DOF selected for zero-degree turning radius and lateral movement capability, essential for dance performance.

Body Shape: Rectangular vs. Hexagonal

Rectangular vs hexagonal body. Rectangular chosen for dual-rider seating arrangement and 3 ft × 3 ft shipping constraint compatibility.

Microcontroller Selection

Arduino vs ESP32 vs STM32. ESP32 selected for built-in Bluetooth and paired with Raspberry Pi for high-level computation.

Controller Design

Custom vs premade controller. Custom selected for hexapod-specific controls (gait selection, speed dial) and reliable ESP32-to-ESP32 Bluetooth link.
